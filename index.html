    Step 2

    How the score is calculated

    A transparent formula — no black box, no vibes-based rating.

    01

    Point mapping

    Each option maps to a point value:

    • A0
    • B1
    • C2
    • D3
    • E4
    02

    The formula

    Six scored categories, 24 maximum points.

    rating = (sum_of_points / max_points) * 100

    Experience is captured for context only and never enters the score.

    03

    Role-based weighting

    • Architect / Tech Lead (D): 1.2× multiplier on AI Project Management.
    • Student / Fresh Grad (F or G): Projects category is excluded from the denominator (max drops to 20) so you are not penalized for not having shipped 3+ AI projects.
    04

    Peer benchmark

    Your score is compared against a calibrated distribution by role, so you can see where you stand relative to your peers — not against a flat global average.

    FAQ

    Quick answers

    Is my data sent anywhere?

    No. The diagnostic runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is transmitted, stored on a server, or used to train a model.

    Why is Experience not scored?

    Experience is a context modifier — not a competency. We use it to calibrate your peer benchmark, not to inflate or deflate your rating.

    Why do Architects get a Projects multiplier?

    For Architects and Tech Leads, the ability to scope, lead, and ship AI-driven projects is disproportionately predictive of long-term resilience — so we weight it accordingly (1.2×).

    Can I integrate this into my own product?

    Yes. The diagnostic emits a clean JSON payload after each run — pipe it into a progress bar, a dashboard, a Slack bot, or an automated coaching workflow.
